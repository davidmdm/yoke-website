Gitops: Yoke & ArgoCD

The yoke CLI is analagous to helm's. It is is a client-side tool that communicates with your cluster and keeps track of packages deployed to it. However, for many that is not how we deploy packages anymore, helm or not. Where the words Platform Engineering are uttered GitOps is sure to follow.

To that end, we want to be able to declare our Flights as static resources that a controller such as ArgoCD will pick up, and then manage our resources for us. Out of the box ArgoCD supports helm, jsonnette, and kustomize. It also allows you to extend its behavior using config management plugins.
